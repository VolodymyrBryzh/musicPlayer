Monochrome Player v0.1.2

CONTROLS:
- F11 or Double-click background: Toggle fullscreen
- Escape: Exit fullscreen
- Scan All: Find all music in parent folder
- Select Folder: Choose specific folder

SETUP:
1. Put music files (MP3, FLAC, WAV) next to the app or in subfolders
2. Put background images (PNG, JPG) in /backgrounds folder
3. Run monochrome-player.exe

FEATURES:
- Three color themes: Monochrome, Cover Accent, Adaptive
- Custom backgrounds
- Audio visualizer
- Play queue with shuffle/repeat
