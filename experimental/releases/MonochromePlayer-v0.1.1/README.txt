Monochrome Player v0.1.1

USAGE:
1. Put your music files (MP3, FLAC, WAV, etc.) next to the app or in subfolders
2. Put background images (PNG, JPG, WEBP) into the 'backgrounds' folder
3. Run monochrome-player.exe
4. Click 'Scan All' to find all music, or 'Select Folder' to choose specific folder

FEATURES:
- Three color themes: Monochrome, Cover Accent, Adaptive
- Custom backgrounds from /backgrounds folder
- Folder-based library with recursive scan
- Audio visualizer
- Play queue with shuffle and repeat
